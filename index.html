BEGINNINGS

Sonja started at an early age with classical ballet and gymnastics. Fully qualified classical modern dancer and teacher. Fitness and Body art qualifications. Worked and trained world wide as a dancer and teacher (Paris,New York,Chicago just to name some!). Owner of her own gym,dance,musical and circus academy for 15 years in Mallorca,Spain. 15 years experience as a personal teacher (don't like trainer!). Worked as a choreographer in different theatres (west side story and 7brides for 7brothers just to name two of her major productions in Manoel Theatre in Malta). Choreographed several musicals in Spain...(kids shows like Toy Story,Nemo,Tarzan etc.)

PASSION AND GROWTH

Through having that vast experience of owning her own gym with 20 teachers being employed offering the whole range of fitness classes like yoga,Pilates,spinning,Taebo,body pump etc.the inspiration was always there to create her own concept based on her own knowledge of being a dancer and a gymnast and together with knowing the benefits of the different fitness techniques which already existed,she melted them all together in a pot and born was what we know now as the Elasko system. Listening carefully to her clients over the years she knew what they wanted and what kind of lifestyle they were aiming for. The passion to grow her own system became so big that she sold her business and concentrated entirely for 2 years non stop together with her clients on perfect-ionising it and opened in 2015 the first Elasko PT studio(called PUR3FIT)in Mallorca offering exclusively her concept and product which is now run successfully by two master teachers having currently a waiting list for new clients.
